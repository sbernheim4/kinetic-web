What Is Kinetic Global

We are a network of student-run social innovation incubators operating on college campuses across the US. Students have an increasing desire to design and implement creative nonpartisan solutions to pressing social issues. Kinetc Global provides resources to help them and turns potential into action.


Our teams use a three phase process of research, design, and implementation to support action.

Research

Teams start the Kinetic process with in-depth research to thoroughly understand the issue and to identify the gaps or pain points where they can design solutions.

Design

With the knowledge gained from the research phase, teams synthesize the information, brainstorm ideas, and prototype possible solutions to the issue.

Implementation

Teams refine their solution and deliver their pitches to partner organizations, so that together they can implement their ideas and create lasting change.

About Us

Kinetic was founded to help ambitious students impact real change in their local area. What began as a group of 15 college students seeking more effective ways to address social issues has expanded into a network of students in chapters across the US, working within and across communities to improve the world around them.


The Kinetic Global Network provides these students with resources and a starting point upon which to build their models. The Network provides access to mentor advice, Kineticâ€™s easy-to-follow methodology, digital resources, and other like-minded students in Universities across the country.


Ultimately, Kinetic Global envisions a culture of boldly engaged citizens who combine empathy, analysis, and action to create innovative solutions to social issues.

Sponsored Causes

The power of Kinetic Global Network is in the number of motivated college students working on the same issues so that they may serve as resources to learn from each other. As such, Kinetic Global has selected three sponsored issues that have shown a great deal of interest among our members.

These issues, homelessness, food insecurity, and environmental sustainability are pervasive issues meaning that they affect a large number of people in all regions of the country. Kinetic Global has compiled databases of mentors and experts on these issues that will help facilitate your understanding of the issue during the research phase and offer guidance and advice on your team's path towards implementation.
