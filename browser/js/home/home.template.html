What Is Kinetic Global

We are a network of student-run social innovation incubators operating on college campuses across the US. Students have an increasing desire to design and implement creative nonpartisan solutions to pressing social issues. Kinetc Global provides the resources and structure to empower students to transform their ideas from potential to action.


Our teams use a three phase process of research, design, and implementation.

Research

Teams start the Kinetic process with in-depth research to thoroughly understand the issue and to identify the gaps or pain points where they can design solutions.

Design

With the knowledge gained from the research phase, teams synthesize the information, brainstorm ideas, and prototype possible solutions to the issue.

Implementation

Teams refine their solution and deliver their pitches to partner organizations, so that together they can implement their ideas and create lasting change.

About Us

Kinetic was founded to help college students move beyond theoretical conversations to meaningful action. Kinetic began at Williams College with 15 college students seeking more effective ways to address social issues. It has expanded into a national network of college students, in which chapters across the US are working to affect positive, tangible change in their communities.


The Kinetic Global Network provides students with the structure and resources to carry out a three-part process of research, design, and implementation. The Network provides access to an expert network, mentorship, a tested methodology, valuable digital resources and a network of smart and passionate fellow social innovators at universities across the country.


Ultimately, Kinetic Global envisions a culture of boldly engaged citizens who combine empathy, analysis, and action to create innovative solutions to social issues

Key Issues

The power of the Kinetic Global Network is in the number of motivated college students working on the same issues and learning from each other in the process. For this reason, Kinetic Global has selected three core issues that have interested our members in the past.

These issues - homelessness, food insecurity, and environmental sustainability- affect a large number of people in all regions of the country, making them critical issues to address.
